Cookies

G13 uses cookies to help G13 identify and track visitors, their usage of the G13 Websites, and their website access preferences. G13 visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using G13 Websites, with the drawback that certain features of G13 Websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

This Privacy Policy covers the use of cookies by G13 and does not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers.
